CForgotten in a Detention Center for Migrants in Italy

Due to the pandemic, a Tunisian man living in Italy, Nadhimi Touir, lost his job and residence permit. Arrested last November, he was sent to the detention center for migrants located in Gradisca d’Isonzo, northern Italy. His cell had no mattresses or blankets, and the winter wind came in through a broken window. The Gradisca Permanence Center for Repatriation (Cpr) was reopened in January 2020 after being closed for three years due to a riot. Since then, two detainees have died. Touir is still waiting to be deported or released. “This place makes you crazy. We are not human anymore.”.

Tools: reporting
Language: English, Italian
